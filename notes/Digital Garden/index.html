Muliama's Garden

[[Digital Garden]]

It's a living document that evolve with you. A place to document projects and knowledge. It could also help you store events of your life.

Useful way to share information on the internet if you want to Learn In Public. Don't follow "personal blog" conventions. It's more a work-in-progress wiki. Each pages are always a work in progress. The goal isn't to get everything right. The goal is it experiment, test ideas and get feedback.

You do a little bit everyday. Don't try to publish finished pieces. Just plant little ideas around. Then water them everyday.

"gardens make their imperfection known to readers."- Maggie Appleton

It's a place to be yourself. A safe place for you to learn. A place for you to expose your ideas.
