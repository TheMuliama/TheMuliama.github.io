Digital Garden

It's a living document that evolve with you. A place to write about projects and knowledge. It could also help you store events of your life.

A way to share information if you want to Learn In Public. Don't follow "personal blog" conventions. See it as a work-in-progress wiki. Each pages are always a work-in-progress. The goal isn't to get everything right. The goal is it experiment, test ideas and get feedback.

You do a little bit everyday. Don't try to publish finished pieces. Just plant little ideas around. Then water them everyday.

"gardens make their imperfection known to readers."- Maggie Appleton

It's a place to be yourself. A safe place for you to learn. A place for you to expose your ideas.
