Chapter 1 Summary

  • Habits are the compound interest of self-improvement. Getting 1 percent better every day counts a lot in the long-run.
  • Habits are double edged swords. They can work for you or against you, so understanding the details of habit formation is important.
  • Small changes often appear to make no difference until you cross a critical threshold. You need to ==be patient== in order to get the outcome of a process.
  • An atomic habit is a little habit that is part of a larger system, they are the building blocks of remarkable results.
