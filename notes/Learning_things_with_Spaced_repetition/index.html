[[Learning things with Spaced repetition]]

I was looking into one of my Obsidian plugin documentation

  • A great introduction to flashcard creation
  • A cool interactive comic about how your memory work and the concept of spaced repetition

To get the most out of spaced repetition you must make you cards Small, connected and meaningful.

Small because too much info on a card is bad for learning. Connected to pictures, sounds, context, and/or personal details! But most importantly meaningful by making sure your learning is in service of doing something you care about.
