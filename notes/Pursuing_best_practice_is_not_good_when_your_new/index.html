Do not pursue best practice when your new

https://dev.to/theodinproject/pursuing-best-practices-is-a-bad-practice-when-youre-new-37pb

Don't try to apply professional principles, try out things then learn from your mistakes. When trying to apply professional principles when new to a subject it prevent experimenting.

We are pushed to "don't even try unless you know the answer." We want to be right and scared that everyone think of us as fool. That's why we try to apply every pro principles when learning.

"Skills in a thing are an outcome of regular practice over time."
